About

My name is Yu Ke Ying. I go by Yukagi because it is a lot easier to pronounce and remember. It's a play on my name, where I rearrange 'Ke Ying' as 'Key ing'. From there, I used the word 'Key' and translated it into Japanese, which is how I got 'Kagi' Therefore, putting my surname together with it gives me 'Yukagi" I thought of this name at like, 3am. I know it lacks creativity and its not funny at all, I just like it and have been using it since I was 16, so i've gotten used to using it.
